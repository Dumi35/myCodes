Techstars is an online educational platform by Fhenix Africa for kids between ages 6-18
where they can access courses, answer quizzes, attend online classes on Google Meets
and many more all from the comfort of their homes.

It provides a range of 5 various courses: Front end web dev, Back end web dev, 
Scratch animation, UI/UX design, and Basic ICT.

It uses a subscription-based plan where users will have to pay a fee of N30,000 to 
register and continuously pay this same fee every 3 months to keep accessing the 
website's resources.

Though Basic ICT would be free for the first month.
